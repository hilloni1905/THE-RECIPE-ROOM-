Mille-Feuille (Napoleon)

Mille-feuille, also known as a Napoleon or custard slice, is a classic French pastry that consists of layers of flaky puff pastry, alternating with layers of pastry cream (crème pâtissière). The name "mille-feuille" translates to "a thousand layers" in French, which reflects the numerous thin and crispy layers of pastry created through a process of rolling, folding, and baking.

Prep Time

15-20 mins

Cook Time

20-30 mins

Serving

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

Bake puff pastry sheets according to the package instructions until golden and puffed.

Step 2

Let them cool, then cut them into equal-sized rectangles.

Step 3

Layer puff pastry with pastry cream and repeat, creating a stack.

Step 4

Dust the top with powdered sugar and, if desired, chocolate drizzle.

Ingredients

Puff pastry sheets

Pastry cream (made with milk, sugar, eggs, and vanilla)

Powdered sugar for dusting
