Chickpea, Avocado, & Feta Salad

Chickpea, Avocado, and Feta Salad is a popular choice for a healthy, filling meal or side dish. It's known for its combination of textures and flavors, offering creaminess, crunch, and a medley of tastes in every bite. The salad is versatile, and you can adjust the ingredients and dressing to suit your taste preferences. It's a great option for those looking for a balanced and nutritious meal.

Prep Time

10-15 mins

Cook Time

5-10 mins

Serving

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

In a medium bowl, combine chickpeas, avocado, cilantro, green onion, feta cheese, and lime juice. Stir until mixed well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve.

Step 2

If you need the salad to be vegan, you can leave out the feta cheese, it will still be tasty!

Ingredients

Chickpeas– rinsed and drained

Avocados– use ripe avocados!

Cilantro– if you don’t care for cilantro, you can use basil or parsley.

Green onion– red onion would also work!

Feta cheese– it adds a creamy and salty kick to the salad.

Lime juice– squeeze fresh lime juice over the salad for flavor and to prevent the avocado from browning. Lemon juice would also be good!
