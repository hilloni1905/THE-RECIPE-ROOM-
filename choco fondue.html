Chocolate Fondue

Chocolate fondue is a dessert or snack where pieces of food, such as fruit, marshmallows, or cake, are dipped into a pot of melted chocolate. The melted chocolate is typically kept warm using a fondue pot or similar device. It's a fun and interactive way to enjoy chocolate and socialize with friends or family.

Prep Time

10-15 mins

Cooking Time

5-10 mins

Serving

1 to 2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

In a microwave-safe bowl or on the stove, heat the heavy cream until it's hot but not boiling.

Step 2

Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for a minute.

Step 3

Stir the mixture until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth.

Step 4

Transfer the chocolate fondue to a fondue pot or a serving bowl.

Step 5

Serve with an assortment of dippers for dipping.

Ingredients

8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate

1/2 cup heavy cream

Chopped dippers (e.g., strawberries, bananas, marshmallows, cookies)
