Churros with Chocolate Dipping Sauce

Churros are a popular Spanish and Latin American snack made from deep-fried dough. They are typically long, thin sticks of dough, often ridged or twisted, and can be enjoyed plain or coated in sugar. Churros are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, with a slightly sweet flavor. They are commonly served with dipping sauces like chocolate or caramel and are a beloved treat at fairs, festivals, and as a street food. Churros have become popular worldwide and come in various shapes and sizes, but the classic Spanish churro remains a favorite.

Prep Time

15-20 minutes

Cook Time

20-30 minutes

Serving

2 servings

Instructions

Step 1

In a saucepan, combine the water, sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil. Bring it to a boil and remove from heat. Stir in the flour until the mixture forms a ball of dough.

Step 2

In a separate deep saucepan or deep fryer, heat vegetable oil to 375°F (190°C).

Step 3

Transfer the churro dough to a pastry bag fitted with a star tip. You can also use a plastic bag with a small hole cut in the corner. Squeeze out 4-6 inch (10-15 cm) strips of dough directly into the hot oil, cutting them with scissors. Be cautious not to overcrowd the pan.

Step 4

Fry the churros until they are golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.

Step 5

Remove the churros from the oil and drain them on a paper towel to remove excess oil. Combine 1/2 cup of sugar and the cinnamon in a bowl. Roll each churro in the sugar-cinnamon mixture until coated.

Step 6

In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and add the chopped chocolate. Let it sit for a minute to melt, then stir until smooth. Stir in the vanilla extract.

Step 7

Serve the warm churros with the chocolate dipping sauce on the side for dipping.

Ingredients
For the Churros:

1 cup water

2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 cup all-purpose flour

Vegetable oil, for frying

1/2 cup white sugar

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon


For the Chocolate Dipping Sauce:

1/2 cup heavy cream

4 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped

1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
